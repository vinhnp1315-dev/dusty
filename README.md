# 🧹 dusty - Free up disk space with ease

[![](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download_Dusty-Blue-blue.svg)](https://github.com/vinhnp1315-dev/dusty)

## 📁 What is dusty?

Dusty is a tool for your computer. It finds files you do not need and removes them. Your computer gathers temporary files over time. These files take space. Dusty scans your hard drive for this clutter. It keeps your important files safe. You gain more room for photos, videos, and documents.

This app runs in your menu bar. You see it at the top of your screen. You click the icon to start a scan. The app identifies safe items to delete. You choose what to remove. It keeps your system clean.

## ⚙️ System Requirements

Dusty works on modern versions of macOS. You need at least 100MB of free disk space to install the app. Your computer requires an internet connection to perform the initial setup. We suggest keeping your system updated to the latest version of macOS for best results. No other special hardware is necessary.

## 🛠️ How to use the app

Once you launch Dusty, an icon appears in your menu bar. It looks like a small brush.

1. Click the brush icon in your menu bar.
2. Select Scan from the menu.
3. The app checks your disk for safe items.
4. Review the list of files found. 
5. Click Delete to remove the items. 
6. Confirm your choice if the system asks. 

The app clears your temporary files. You see a clear message once the scan finishes. You can quit the app by selecting Quit from the menu.

## 🔒 Safety and Security

Dusty respects your files. It only scans folders known for temporary data. It ignores your personal folders like Documents or Pictures. The app does not transmit your data elsewhere. Everything happens on your machine. You control the scan. You decide what stays and what goes.

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

**Does Dusty slow down my computer?**

No. Dusty runs in the background. It uses very little processor power. You will not notice performance changes while it runs.

**Can I undo a cleaning?**

Dusty moves files to the Trash. You can open your Trash icon to restore files if you change your mind.

**How do I adjust settings?**

Click the gear icon inside the Dusty menu. You can choose which folders to scan. You can also hide the icon if you want to run it without the menu bar. 

**Is this app free?**

Yes. Dusty is open-source software. You can download and use it at no cost.

**What if an error occurs?**

Close the app and restart it. Most issues resolve with a restart. If problems persist, check the download page for updates.
